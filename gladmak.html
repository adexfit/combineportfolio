Overview

This project was done for Gladmak International corporation. Gladmak International Corporation is a USA based corporation that engages in trading, acquisition, brokerage, of bioenergy products, and sale of biomass and agro biomass products for power production, Energy generation and heating purposes.

The goal of the project was to create a responsive business website for Gladmak that would:

  1. increase brand awareness
  2. establish credibility and build trust with customers
  3. help in conveying information to users
  4. increase sales and revenue
  5. improve customer experience

This project lasted for 3 weeks and I was the only designer who worked on it.

My roles

  1. Interaction design
  2. Visual design
  3. wireframing
  4. Information Architecture
  5. Prototyping and Testing

The Process


This project started with a user research which was done by Gladmak to understand their user behaviours and needs. The outcome of this research and other information (e.g business goals) was communicated to me in form of a creative brief which was used for the design process.

Based on the details from the research, I created the user persona and the empathy map. I carried out the ideation process using the "how might we technique", and used Figma for the wireframing and prototyping process.
